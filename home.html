Welcome to Mazapa Cacao!

Mazapa Cacao was founded in 2017, derived from the idea to create brand new stores from Cacep Chocolates and Hacienda Jesus Maria. Our history starts back in 1940s, when our founder, Juan Cacep began to produce raw materials out from Cacao Beans. Our beans are produced in Hacienda Jesus Maria, within our 50 hectareas plantation, organically cultivated.

We now have 5 whole new stores in Mexico:
Monterrey:
    Mazapa Barrio
    Mazapa La Silla
Tabasco:
    Mazapa Via Dos
    Mazapa Morett
    Mazapa Anturios
